Welcome to Thomson's Prime Home Solutions

With Years of Experience

Thomson's Prime Home Solutions is a trusted leader in providing top-quality services for all your construction and home improvement needs. Specializing in a wide range of offerings, including construction, roofing, and house remodeling, we transform spaces with precision and care.

Our expertise extends to window tinting, enhancing energy efficiency and privacy, and shuttering and guttering, ensuring structural stability and effective water drainage. With a commitment to craftsmanship, we excel in concreting, laying a strong foundation for your projects, and painting, adding the perfect finishing touch to your home or commercial space.

House fencing repair involves restoring the structural integrity and appearance of a damaged or worn fence. Common issues include broken panels, leaning posts, rusted metal, or peeling paint, which can compromise privacy, security, and curb appeal. Repairs may include replacing damaged sections, reinforcing posts, treating for rust or rot, and applying fresh paint or stain for protection.
